The Best Movies According to Kavya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1.Mr.Bean

A charming and endearing character, known for his quirky antics and resourceful problem-solving, often finding creative solutions to everyday situations with a childlike sense of wonder.

2.Irumugan

A 2016 Tamil action thriller, sci-fi, and mystery movie, follows a suspended RAW agent who returns to track down a nemesis who has developed a super drug, with Vikram shining in a dual role as the hero and villain.

3.Karnan

A powerful and poignant Tamil film that tells the story of a young man from a marginalized community who fights for the rights of his people against systemic oppression, exploring themes of caste, injustice, and the struggle for dignity.
